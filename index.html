About Us
The "Automir" company was founded in Slovakia in 2006 by former employees of the largest tyre enterprises in Ukraine, JSC "Dneproshina" and JSC "Rosava" and began its activity as an operator of the EU tyre market, promoting in its majority the products of PA "Dneproshina" and other tyres factories organized on the territory of the former USSR. The headquarters are located in the city of Trenčín. The Trenčín Region is a tyre region owing to such industrial tyre giants as “Matador”, “Vipo”, “Konstrukta” industry and others. Automir s.r.o. started developing its business, gaining experience and building up the partnerships, strengthening its position in the market and winning the authority amount customers. The market situation began to change over the time creating a shortage of certain tyres models and an urgent need arose to support partners companies with the required tyres sizes. A new project had started and shortly the Company produced tyres under the own Trade Mark. This is how new brands were introduced to the market, such as: “UkrTechProm”, “SuperTechProm”. In 2010, the first own agricultural tyre size 23.1R26, was produced and successfully supplied to the European market. This was only the first step, as the market was already demanding further moves to expand the number of manufactured sizes. The challenge was great but the Company had qualified tyre specialists, so the goal had been achieved. At that time, the production of tyres 21.3R24 and 16 / 70-20 had begun. Nowadays company produces 24 types of tyres amount them are passenger tyres, agricultural, truck tyres (including truck tyres with adjustable pressure for military vehicles) as well as industrial tyres. Our tyres have proven themselves in the markets of Ukraine, Europe (Bulgaria, Germany, Hungary, France, Netherlands) also in China and Egypt. Automir s.r.o. is a European partner of the Ukrainian tyres corporation “Ukrteсhprom”, founded in 1993. The company enters a new stage and planning to participate in public procurements in European Union and in Slovakia. Also Automir started collaboration and distribution with largest world tyre’s manufacturers and distribute their product in Slovakia, Europe and Ukraine.
