About Production
Group of Companies "Ukrtekhprom" was founded in 1993 in Ukraine and began its activity as a tire market operator, promoting mostly product of JSC "Dneproshina". The enterprises began to work actively, gaining experience and creating partnerships, strengthening their position at the market, gaining credibility with buyers. The market situation began to change over time, there was a shortage of individual tire sizes and a big demand had risen to provide company’s customers with the required tire sizes. The Company started to work on the production of tires under its own Trade Mark. This is how the brands new brands were introduced to the market such as UkrTechProm, SuperTechProm, and the tires produced under these brands on the basis of the facilities of JSC Rosava In 2010, the first tire in size 23.1R26 was produced. This was only the first step and the market was already demanding to move forward and to expand the number of produced sizes. The goal was difficult but the company had qualified tire specialists and this task was completed successfully. Currently the company produces 24 tires sizes including: passenger, agricultural, truck, truck with adjustable pressure, industrial tires. The tires have proven themselves well both at the markets of Ukraine and in Europe (Bulgaria, Germany, Hungary, France, the Netherlands), China and Egypt. The new tire size 500 / 50R17 was launched in April 2021. Tubeless tire for tractor trailers and trailed agricultural machines.
