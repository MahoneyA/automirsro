UkrTechProm
The Group of Companies UkrTechProm was founded in the Dnipro city, Ukraine in 1993 and began activities as a tyre market operator, generally promoting the tyres produced by JSC Dneproshina, plant.
UkrTechProm is one of the leading tyre market operators in Ukraine and is the official dealer of the following manufacturers and brands:


- Rosava, Rosava-AgroS, Premiorri.

- Cooper Tires

- Pirelli

- Continental AG

- Matador

- Tigar Tyres

- Triangle

- Nokian Tyres

- Goodyear Tire

- TITAN Tire

- Petlas

- Greforce

- Kapsen

- Armour Lande

-Radburg

The Company offers to its clients a wide range of products such as: passenger, light truck, agricultural and truck tyres, wheels, oils and batteries. Today The Group of Companies UkrTechProm has branches and warehouses in 10 regions of Ukraine among their clients are the largest legal entities and individuals.
The Head Office is located in Dnipro and includes a warehouse complex with a total area of 10,000 м². Starting from 2005, a chain of Company's regional offices has been operating throughout Ukraine, located in largest cities and industrial centers of the country:

Kyiv, Kharkiv, Odesa, Mykolaiv, Poltava, Zaporizhzhia, Khmelnytskyi, Vinnytsia,Kropyvnytskyi, Kryvyi Rih, Bila Tserkva

UkrTechProm also offers online services to its client through the online shop UTP.COM.UA
